Avenged Sevenfold

Like Muse, Avenged Sevenfold (A7X) is very hard to fit into a single category. Each album sounds unique and unlike any other band. On surface level, they may seem like every other rock band, but delve a little deeper and you will see how vulnerable they are with their audience and just how relatable the songs can be.
